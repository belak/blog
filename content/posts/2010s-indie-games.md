## My Favorite Indie Games of the 2010s by Year

When I started building this list, I originally started ranking everything, but I really can't so many of these games are amazing... and forcing myself to rank them would be a disservice to the games themselves.

Many of these games I started collecting through the Humble Indie Bundles. Originally, that was the easiest way for me to discover new games (as I was in college and didn't have time or money to buy many games).

I'll try to say a few words about each of the games on my list, what I played it on, and why I chose it.

### 2010

All these games were played on PC and were most likely obtained through Humble Bundles. Seemed to be a great year for platformers.

- Limbo
- Super Meat Boy
- VVVVVV

### 2011

This is the first year it seemed like games focused on exploration were taking off. It's one of my favorite trends throughout the decade. I'm glad this is a theme that has been given focus.
